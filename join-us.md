## Join the Band!

Are you a musician looking for a friendly and active group to play with? The Maidenhead Concert Band welcomes new members of all ages and skill levels. We are particularly interested in players of [mention specific instruments if needed, e.g., brass, woodwind, percussion], but all instruments are considered.

We rehearse at [Furze Platt Senior School](https://maps.app.goo.gl/yCiUrALqvkQENGLx6) on Monday evenings from **7:30 PM to 9:00** PM. Our rehearsals are a great opportunity to improve your playing, meet new people, and contribute to our vibrant sound.

If you're interested in joining, please feel free to come along to a rehearsal or contact us beforehand for a chat. We'd love to hear from you!

No auditions required – just a love for music and a willingness to participate!
