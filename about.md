## About Us

The Maidenhead Concert Band is a community music group. Our aim is to provide a welcoming and supportive environment for musicians of all ages and abilities to come together, share their love for music, and perform for the local community. We 

Our voluntary nature means we are not-for-profit and all efforts are focused on the music and the enjoyment of our members and audiences. We are always looking for new ways to engage with the community.

### Rehearsals

Rehearsals are held at [Furze Platt Senior School](https://maps.app.goo.gl/yCiUrALqvkQENGLx6) each Monday from 19:30 - 21:00 and follow the school term schedule:


* Spring term - 12th January - 23rd March (excl half term - 16th Feb)

* Summer term - 13th April -  17th July (excl bank holiday on 4th May & half term on 25th May)

* Winter term - 7th September - 7th December (excl half term - 26th Oct)
