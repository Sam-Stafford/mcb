Welcome to the Maidenhead Concert Band

The Maidenhead Concert Band was formed by father and daughter, Peter Ripper and Carrie in January 1998 to enable musicians of all ages to get together and play their instruments. The band is run on a voluntary basis. We play at many local fundraising events such as The Maidenhead Carnival, Twyford Donkey Derby, Littlewick Show to name a few, as well as putting on our own charity concerts at Norden Farm, Altwood Road, Maidenhead.

We rehearse at Furze Platt Senior School on Monday evenings from 7.30pm to 9pm
