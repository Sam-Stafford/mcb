## Welcome to the Maidenhead Concert Band

The Maidenhead Concert Band was formed by father and daughter, Peter Ripper and Carrie in January 1998 to enable musicians of all ages to get together and play their instruments. The band is run on a voluntary basis, driven by a passion for music and community spirit.

We play at many local fundraising events such as The Maidenhead Carnival, Twyford Donkey Derby, Littlewick Show to name a few, as well as putting on our own charity concerts at Norden Farm in Maidenhead. Our performances bring joy to audiences and support worthy causes in our local area.
