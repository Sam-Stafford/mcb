Welcome to the Maidenhead Concert Band

The Maidenhead Concert Band was formed by father and daughter, Peter Ripper and Carrie in January 1998 to enable musicians of all ages to get together and play their instruments. The band is run on a voluntary basis, driven by a passion for music and community spirit.

We play at many local fundraising events such as The Maidenhead Carnival, Twyford Donkey Derby, Littlewick Show to name a few, as well as putting on our own charity concerts at Norden Farm, Altwood Road, Maidenhead. Our performances bring joy to audiences and support worthy causes in our local area.

Join us for our next performance or come along to a rehearsal!

About Us

The Maidenhead Concert Band is more than just a musical group; we are a community. Our mission is to provide a welcoming and supportive environment for musicians of all ages and abilities to come together, share their love for music, and perform for the local community.

From our humble beginnings in 1998, we have grown into a cherished local institution, known for our diverse repertoire and enthusiastic performances. We believe in the power of music to connect people and enrich lives, and we are proud to contribute to the cultural fabric of Maidenhead.

Our voluntary leadership ensures that all efforts are focused on the music and the enjoyment of our members and audiences. We are always looking for new ways to engage with the community and expand our musical horizons.

Join the Band!

Are you a musician looking for a friendly and active group to play with? The Maidenhead Concert Band welcomes new members of all ages and skill levels. We are particularly interested in players of [mention specific instruments if needed, e.g., brass, woodwind, percussion], but all instruments are considered.

We rehearse at Furze Platt Senior School on Monday evenings from 7:30 PM to 9:00 PM. Our rehearsals are a great opportunity to improve your playing, meet new people, and contribute to our vibrant sound.

If you're interested in joining, please feel free to come along to a rehearsal or contact us beforehand for a chat. We'd love to hear from you!

No auditions required – just a love for music and a willingness to participate!
